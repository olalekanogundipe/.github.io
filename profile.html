My Latest book

The Infinite Enterprise is a manifesto on building systems that outlast their creators. It explores the architecture of permanence — how to design organizations, institutions, and technologies that endure beyond individual lifetimes, leadership transitions, and market cycles.

This is not a book about AI. It's about the deeper question: What does it mean to build something that matters? Drawing from experiences building in resource-constrained environments, the book argues that true longevity comes from designing for human dignity, institutional resilience, and intergenerational continuity.
